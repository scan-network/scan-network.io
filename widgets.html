Benefits of joining SCAN

  • ACCESS TO OPEN DIRECTORY: All the projects and experts from the SCAN expertise directory.
  • CONTRIBUTE/ACCESS TO THE NEWSLETTER: You would be welcome to contribute any interesting news items to the quarterly SCAN newsletter. This newsletter is freely distributed to the SCAN members. If you have subscribed to the newsletter, then you will get a copy of the newsletter with community news and events.
  • ATTEND FREE EVENTS: You will receive invites to attend regular events organized by the SCAN network. Most of these events will be virtual with occasional in-person events. The events will be free to attend.
